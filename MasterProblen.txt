1️⃣ Payment Processing System – System Design (Typical Fintech Interview)

Problem

Design a high-scale payment processing platform similar to what companies like Mastercard or Visa operate.

Key Requirements

Functional

Accept payment requests

Validate card / payment details

Process transaction

Handle success/failure

Notify client


Non-Functional

High availability

Low latency

Fault tolerance

Idempotency (no duplicate payments)

Scalability
